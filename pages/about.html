About DigiLit

DigiLit is a front-end search engine powered by IMDB, Letterboxd, and MyAnimeList (MAL). DigiLit is designed to search up any piece of digital literature (hence the name), such as
live action or animated television shows and movies. This project was created to help teach myself html/css.

To use, simply enter the name of a tv show or movie and click the button that corresponds to whichever site you would like to search on. If you hit enter (and do not click any buttons),
you will automatically be redirected to IMDB.
